Privacy Policy – Ubyflow

Last updated: February 13, 2026

1. Introduction

Ubycohub Limited ("we", "us", "our") operates the Ubyflow mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy in compliance with the Nigeria Data Protection Regulation (NDPR) and other applicable laws.

2. Information We Collect

We collect:

  • Personal information: full name, phone number, email, BVN, NIN, date of birth, selfie (for KYC)
  • Financial information: wallet balances, transaction history
  • Device & usage data: IP address, device ID, OS version, app usage patterns
  • KYC documents: government-issued ID (when required for higher limits)

3. How We Collect Information

  • Directly from you during registration, KYC, and transactions
  • Automatically from your device and app usage
  • From third parties (Smile ID for verification, payment gateways for transaction data)

4. How We Use Your Information

To:

  • Provide and improve the App's services
  • Verify your identity and comply with KYC/AML laws
  • Process transactions and send confirmations/receipts
  • Prevent fraud and secure the platform
  • Send service-related notifications (OTP, updates)
  • (With consent) send marketing messages

5. Sharing Your Information

We may share data with:

  • Service providers (Smile ID, payment gateways, SMS providers like Termii)
  • Regulatory authorities (CBN, NDPC, law enforcement) when required
  • Business partners for service delivery (never sold to marketers without consent)

6. Data Security

We use industry-standard measures (encryption, access controls, regular audits) to protect your data. However, no system is 100% secure.

7. Your Rights (under NDPR)

You have the right to:

  • Access, correct, or delete your data
  • Object to processing
  • Withdraw consent
  • Lodge a complaint with the Nigeria Data Protection Commission

To exercise these rights, email: privacy@ubyflow.com

8. Data Retention

We keep your data only as long as necessary for the purposes above or as required by law (typically 5–7 years for financial records).

9. International Transfers

Some data may be processed outside Nigeria (e.g. cloud servers). We ensure adequate safeguards are in place.

10. Children

The App is not intended for persons under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

11. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. We will notify you of material changes via the App or email.
